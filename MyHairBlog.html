HOME

My name is Glory Kendi Kiruja, a 23-year-old Kenyan lass, and I am here to

offer an alternative approach to beauty. On this website, I write articles primarily

on natural hairstyles and post pictures of my own braided or pinned hair. I

recreate popular hairdos from movies and television shows, and I regularly come

up with a content, which I love sharing with you!

 

ABOUT

Going natural is more difficult than it should be, but with the help of my website,

today and read on for all the inspiration you will ever need to keep switching

things up.
